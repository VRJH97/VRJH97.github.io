About This Publication

Science explained
without losing its depth

This is a publication for people who want to understand science as a system — not just its conclusions. Every schematic here is built from primary sources, real database architectures, and the actual infrastructure that makes modern science possible.

The goal is not to simplify. It is to make visible what is usually invisible — the networks, the pipelines, the global agreements that allow a mutation discovered in London to change a treatment protocol in Tokyo within a year.

64+
Scientific databases mapped, annotated, and linked across 17 disciplines — from genomics to botany.
